About

XTENSION

Founder / Director of XTENSION

I have successfully attained a Bachelor of Arts degree in Cinema and Drama, with a specialization in Directing, from the National University of Arts and Culture located in Yangon, Burma. I've been working as a Director since 2018, overseeing music videos, commercial digital videos, TVCs, animation videos, corporate videos, and various other productions.

I am also passionate about editing and color grading. At XTENSION, we offer comprehensive services in pre-production, production, and post-production. Based on my directing projects, such as "Kyay Zue Par Kwal" (Shine), "Living Legend" (Player K), and "The High Ground" (Bunny Phyoe & J Me), have achieved million hits in the industry. Let's join forces to turn imagination into reality together!
